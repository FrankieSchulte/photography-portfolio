I’m usually looking for the moment around the obvious one.

The laugh after the posed photo. The room settling before a speech. The second a stage light catches someone just right.

My work moves between graduations, gatherings, live shows, and personal trips. The subjects change, but I’m usually looking for the same thing—people and places that still feel like themselves in the frame.

I want the process to feel straightforward. I’ll offer direction when it’s useful, stay out of the way when it isn’t, and make sure we know what matters before the day begins.
